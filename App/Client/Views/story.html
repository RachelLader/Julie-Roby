Roby and Julie’s Love Story

The story began in 2006 at TJ High School. Julie was a sophomore, Roby a junior, and they met through mutual friends. Julie and Roby enjoyed dating through high school- going to school dances, attending each other’s sporting events and going out for sushi. In 2007 Roby graduated from TJ and went to play soccer at University of Richmond, while Julie graduated in 2008 and went to swim at University of Maryland.

Long distance and opposing seasons made dating in college difficult, but this time apart was valuable in personal growth. While finishing college, Roby experienced the passing of his mother, which proved to be challenging for their relationship and his family. Through this time, Roby chose to move back to Maryland, which brought him and Julie even closer.

Julie and Roby have supported each other, both personally and professionally throughout their transition from college into the working world. Living in New Market, Maryland, Roby and Julie happily adopted their puppy, Maddox, in the spring of 2014. On July 1, 2015, Roby took Julie on a picnic and asked her to be his forever. Through all the tears, Julie said yes!

Roby and Julie are happy to join the 2% of couples who marry their high school sweetheart. No matter the distance, odds or circumstances, when they got to see each other again everything fell back into place. The theme for their wedding is “it was always you,” and they can’t wait to embark on this new journey together.
